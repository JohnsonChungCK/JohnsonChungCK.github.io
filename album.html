Rice Planting
Traditional rice planting is disappearing in Hong Kong. However, rice field which is a kind of wetland habitat is important habitat for migratory birds. It provides birds with food and roosting space. One of significant habitat management work is rice planting.
Rice Harvest
Golden rice field attracts some critically endangered bird species to visit. We leave part of the rice for birds. We also organize activiities for general public in order to educate our next generations. They learn how to harvest rice and the value of wetland conservation. Harvesting is the happest process of rice planting!
Bird Survey
I can do bird survey individually. Bird species, abundance, behaviour and their habitat are recorded for scientific study or habitat monitoring. The diversity and abundance of bird can reflect the "health" of the environment.

Black-faced Spoonbill
Black-faced Spoonbill is an endangered species (IUCN red list). An iconic wetland "star" in Hong Kong. They also distribute in Korean, China, Taiwan and some south-east asian countries.
